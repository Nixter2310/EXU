Welcome to Exandria Unlimited!

Welcome to the next chapter in the Critical Role universe...Exandria Unlimited! This mini-series tells the story of five budding adventurers as they travel together through Exandria on various quests. The game is run by Critical Role's newest GM, Aabria Iyengar. Find out more about the characters and the actors that play them in the 'Characters' section. The series airs live on Twitch and YouTube every Thursday and the video is available on Critical Role's YouTube channel every Monday.
